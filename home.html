522880: My wonderful hobby

It is called rock climbing.

While the concept of rocks and differences in height might seem novel to the dutch, these are in fact things that exist.
If you combine both of those concepts, and you try and get up that combined concept, then you get rock climbing.

It is a bit like the dutch sport Fierljeppen, except instead of a ditch, to get over, you have a rock, to get onto, and instead of a pole, you use your hands.
